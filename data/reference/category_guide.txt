=== NYS COUNTY BUDGET CATEGORY QUICK REFERENCE ===

This guide maps common query terms to the correct LEVEL_1_CATEGORY and LEVEL_2_CATEGORY values
in the clean_county_expenditures.csv and clean_county_revenue.csv datasets.

--- USEFUL QUERY PATTERNS ---

To analyze legislative board spending:
  Filter: level_1_category = 'General Government', level_2_category = 'Administration'
  Or: account_code_narrative LIKE '%Legislative%'

To analyze highway/transportation spending:
  Filter: level_1_category = 'Transportation'

To compare counties:
  Group by: county, level_1_category, sum(amount)

To analyze trends over time:
  Group by: calendar_year, level_1_category, sum(amount)

To find the largest spending categories:
  Group by: level_1_category, sum(amount), order by sum desc
